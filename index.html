About This Workshop

When a Builder Solves His Own Problem

This workshop is an opportunity to sit with Bakari Holmes — a software engineer, educator, and musician who has spent his career at the intersection of technology and teaching — and hear how he turned a personal frustration with inconsistent hiring practices into Interview Rubric Creator, an AI-powered tool that transforms job descriptions into structured interview rubrics.

InterviewRubric.com is grounded in authentic assessment — the academic discipline of creating evaluations that measure actual competence using clear, observable criteria. Whether you’re actively interviewing, preparing to return to the market, or curious about how AI can eliminate guesswork from hiring, this session is for you.

Bakari didn’t just build this for others — he built it because he needed it himself. That’s the kind of builder story that resonates with every engineer who has ever sat on either side of a broken interview process.

Your Guest Speaker

Bakari Holmes

Bakari Holmes
Software Engineer  ·  Educator  ·  Founder, Interview Rubric Creator

Bakari Holmes is a full-stack software engineer with dual degrees in Physics and Software Engineering and a career that spans some of the most recognizable names in tech — including 23andMe, Roblox, Course Hero, and Accenture. He describes his approach as “empathetic engineering — engineering that has at its heart the needs and wants of others.”

Before entering industry, Bakari was a STEM Master Teacher in Palo Alto and a physics teacher in Salinas, CA. That background in education and graduate research in authentic assessment directly informs his product: he recognized that grading and interviewing are fundamentally the same challenge — both must answer “can this person actually do the thing?” and both fail when evaluation standards exist only in an evaluator’s mind.

Today, Bakari continues to teach as an Adjunct Instructor at CodePath (Georgia State University), mentors aspiring engineers through App Academy, and writes the “Tech and AI Real Talk” newsletter on Substack. He is also a 7× award-winning soul and jazz vocalist, arranger, and producer.
